Our Approach
Globally Sourced
The best brands and technology breakout beyond their own borders to become global leaders. So we scour the world for 25-30 leading multi-billion-market-cap companies.
Forward-Looking
Over time, we invest more and more into the same businesses. Thus, we select for business models that can continue building market share for years to come.
Rooted in Value
We ensure that our entry multiples afford a high probability for future share appreciation, and seek out companies that can generate a double digit return on newly invested capital.
