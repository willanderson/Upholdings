Founder, CIO
Robert Cantwell founded Upholdings in 2019 to develop benchmark beating investment strategies.
Prior to Upholdings, Robert built and led the finance and operations departments for Everlane, a modern brand offering ethically-sourced apparel over the web.

As an investor, Robert has spent his career under the instruction of Roger McNamee, former head of the top performing T. Rowe Price Science & Technology Fund, and founder of Silver Lake and Elevation Partners. Roger is both an investor and advisor to Upholdings.
